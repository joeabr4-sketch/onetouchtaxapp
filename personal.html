📚 Tax Basics — What Every Employee Needs to Know
📄
What is an IRP5?
Your employer's summary of what you earned and how much tax was deducted. You need this to file.
🏥
Medical Aid Credit
If you pay medical aid, SARS gives you a monthly credit — R364 for you, R364 for your spouse, R246 per child.
🏦
Retirement Annuity
RA contributions reduce your taxable income — up to 27.5% of your income, max R350,000/year.

✅ Document Checklist
Gather these before you start eFiling. Tick each one off as you get it.
Essential — Everyone Needs These
Required
IRP5 Certificate from your employer
Your employer must provide this. Request it from HR or payroll if not received by June.
SARS eFiling login details
Register at efiling.sars.gov.za if you haven't already. You'll need your ID number.
South African bank account details
SARS pays refunds directly into your bank account. Make sure your details are updated on eFiling.
Deductions — Get More Back
Increases refund
Medical Aid Tax Certificate (IT3(f))
Your medical aid scheme issues this. Download it from their member portal. Shows annual contributions.
Retirement Annuity Certificate (IT3(f))
From your RA provider (Sanlam, Discovery, Old Mutual etc.). Confirms annual contributions made.
Home loan interest certificate
From your bank (FNB, ABSA, Nedbank, Standard Bank). Only if you have a home loan — interest may be deductible if renting out.
If You Have Extra Income
May apply to you
Travel logbook (if you receive a car allowance)
Must record every business trip — date, km, destination, purpose. SARS will reject claims without a logbook.
Side income records (freelance / rental)
Any income outside your salary must be declared. Keep invoices and bank records.
Section 18A donation certificates
If you donated to a registered charity, you can claim a deduction. They must issue an official certificate.
📋 eFiling Walkthrough
Follow these steps in order. Click each step when done to mark it complete.
1
Register or log in to SARS eFiling
Go to efiling.sars.gov.za and log in. New user? Click "Register" — you'll need your SA ID number, personal details, and a cellphone number.
👉 Go to: efiling.sars.gov.za
Username: Your ID number or email
Forgot password? Click "Forgot" and SARS will send a reset OTP to your phone.

Once logged in, click this step to mark it done and move to the next one.
2
Check for your Auto-Assessment
SARS may have auto-assessed you. Go to Returns → Income Tax → ITR12. If there's a pre-filled return, review it carefully before accepting.
In eFiling: Returns → Income Tax → ITR12 → Filed Returns

⚠️ Don't just accept the auto-assessment! SARS sometimes misses deductions you're entitled to. Use our Auto-Assessment Checker to verify it first.
3
Request your ITR12 return
If no auto-assessment, click "Request Return" to generate your ITR12. SARS will pre-fill what they know — your job is to complete and verify the rest.
In eFiling: Returns → Income Tax → ITR12 → Request Return

The form will open with pre-filled sections. Work through each section from top to bottom. Don't skip any section — even if it looks blank.
4
Complete the Income section
Enter your employment income from your IRP5. If you have multiple employers, add each one separately. Capture rental or freelance income here too.
What to enter:
• Source Code 3601 = Salary / wages
• Source Code 3605 = Annual bonus
• Source Code 3713 = Car allowance
• Source Code 4210 = Rental income

All of these are on your IRP5 certificate.
5
Add your deductions
This is where you get money back. Add medical aid credits, RA contributions, home office expenses if applicable, and any Section 18A donations.
Deductions checklist:
☐ Medical scheme fees tax credit (auto-calculated from dependants)
☐ Out-of-pocket medical expenses (if over threshold)
☐ Retirement annuity fund contributions
☐ Home office deduction (if working from home — need to calculate floor space %)
☐ Section 18A donations
6
Review, sign and submit
eFiling will show you a tax summary — what you owe or what SARS owes you. Review carefully, sign electronically, and submit. SARS will process within 7–21 working days.
✅ Before you click Submit:
• Does the refund/amount match what you expected?
• Is your bank account correct for refund payment?
• Have you attached all supporting documents?

Once submitted, save your submission receipt number. You'll need it if SARS has questions.
