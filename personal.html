✅ Document Checklist

Gather these before you start eFiling. Tick each as you get it — progress: 0 / 8

SARS eFiling login details
Register at efiling.sars.gov.za if you haven't already. You'll need your SA ID number.
South African bank account details
SARS pays refunds directly to your bank. Ensure your details are updated on eFiling.
IRP5 certificate from your employer
Your employer must provide this by March each year. It shows your salary and PAYE paid.
Medical aid tax certificate (IT3(f))
From your medical aid scheme. Shows total contributions for the tax year.
Retirement annuity certificate (IT3(f))
If you contribute to an RA. From your RA provider — confirms annual contributions.
Travel logbook (if claiming car allowance)
Required by SARS if you receive a car allowance. Must show business km for the year.
Home office calculation (if applicable)
Floor space percentage × annual home costs. Employer must confirm work-from-home arrangement.
Section 18A donation certificates
If you donated to approved public benefit organisations during the tax year.

📋 eFiling Walkthrough

Step-by-step guide to filing your ITR12 on SARS eFiling.

1
Register or log in to SARS eFiling
Go to efiling.sars.gov.za and log in. New user? Click "Register" — you'll need your SA ID number, personal details, and a cellphone number.
👉 efiling.sars.gov.za
2
Check for an auto-assessment
SARS may have pre-assessed you. Go to Returns → Income Tax → ITR12. If there's a pre-filled return, review it carefully. Don't just accept it — SARS sometimes misses deductions you're entitled to.
3
Request your ITR12
If no auto-assessment, click "Request Return". SARS pre-fills what they know — your job is to complete and verify the rest.
In eFiling: Returns → Income Tax → ITR12 → Request Return
4
Capture your income
Enter your salary from your IRP5. Include all income sources: rental, freelance, interest. Match every number to your IRP5 certificate.
5
Add your deductions
Medical aid credits (source code 4116), RA contributions (source code 4001/4006), travel allowance, home office. Each has a specific section in the ITR12.
6
Review, sign and submit
eFiling will show your tax summary — what you owe or what SARS owes you. Check it against our Refund Estimator. Sign electronically and submit. Refund arrives within 7–21 business days.
