  • To provide the OneTouch TaxApp service

These metrics do not contain the content of your messages or AI queries — only a count of events. They are governed by our Fair Usage Policy and are never used for advertising or profiling.

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security — meaning no other user can access your data. All connections use TLS encryption. Access is protected by your authentication credentials.

We share data only with the following processors, each bound by their own data processing agreements and applicable data protection law:

AI Features
AI Usage Policy
Applies to Pro and Full plan features
AI features are optional. They are available on Pro and Full plans only. Free plan users do not have their data processed by AI.

Which Features Use AI

  • Bank statement analysis — your uploaded statement text is sent to Claude AI for categorisation and analysis
  • AI tax assistant — your questions and business context are sent to Claude AI for responses
  • Tax saving tips — your business summary is sent to Claude AI for recommendations

What Data Is Sent

Only the minimum data required to generate results is sent to the AI. For bank analysis this includes statement text. For the assistant this includes your question and basic business profile (type, VAT status). We never send: ID numbers, banking passwords, or full personal profiles.

AI Provider

We use Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's handling of data is governed by their Privacy Policy and Usage Policies.

Accuracy Limitations

AI models can make mistakes. All AI-generated content in OneTouch is:

  • A starting point for your own review — not a final answer
  • Subject to errors, hallucinations, or outdated information
  • Not a substitute for professional tax advice

Bank Statement Analysis Accuracy

The AI is instructed to only extract data it can actually read — it never estimates or invents figures. Where it cannot read a value clearly, it returns blank and flags it for manual review. Despite this, you should always verify the extracted figures against your original bank statement before using them in any tax submission.

Opting Out

You can opt out of AI features at any time by simply not using them. Downgrading to the Free plan removes access to all AI features and no further data is sent to the AI provider.

Monthly AI Call Limits

Pro plan includes 10 AI assistant calls per month. Full plan includes unlimited AI calls. Bank statement analysis calls are separate and not counted against the assistant limit.
