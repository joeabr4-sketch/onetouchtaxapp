Got Questions?

We've got answers.

Is OneTouch a replacement for my accountant? +
No — and we're upfront about that. OneTouch is a compliance tracking and guidance tool. We help you stay on top of deadlines, understand your obligations, and prepare your documents. For complex tax matters, we always recommend working with a registered tax practitioner. Think of us as the organised, always-on assistant your accountant wishes you had.
Is my financial data safe? +
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Bank statement analysis is processed securely and we never sell or share your data with third parties. Your financial information is yours — we're just here to help you make sense of it.
What if I'm not VAT registered? +
No problem. During onboarding, tell us your registration status and we'll only show you the deadlines and obligations that apply to you. If you're not VAT registered, we won't bother you with VAT submissions. The platform is fully personalised to your specific situation.
How accurate is the refund estimator? +
The estimator uses the official 2024/25 SARS tax tables and applies the correct rebates, medical aid tax credits, and RA deductions. It's a reliable estimate — but your actual refund may differ slightly based on your full circumstances. Always verify on eFiling before submitting.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Absolutely. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. You can cancel from your account settings at any time. We'd obviously love for you to stay — but we're confident the product earns its keep every month.
Do you support multiple businesses? +
Yes — on our Premium plan you can manage multiple entities from a single account. This is ideal for business owners with more than one company, or for accountants who want to manage multiple clients. The white-label option also lets accountants brand the platform as their own.
