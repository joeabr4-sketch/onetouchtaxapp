FAQ

Common questions

If you don't find your answer here, our AI assistant is available in the app.

Is OneTouch TaxApp a registered SARS tax practitioner? ▼
No. OneTouch TaxApp is a preparation and organisation tool — not a registered tax practitioner. All documents generated are preparation aids and must be reviewed and submitted by a registered tax practitioner or by yourself via SARS eFiling. We recommend verifying all figures before submitting.
How does the free trial work? ▼
Sign up and get 7 calendar days of full Pro + Full access — no credit card required. You get unlimited invoices, bank reconciliation, payroll, VAT201 prep, and more. After 7 days, choose Pro (R299/mo) or Full (R499/mo) to keep your data and continue. No payment = no access, but your data is always retained.
How does bank reconciliation work? ▼
Export your bank transactions as a CSV or Excel file from your bank's transaction history (FNB, Absa, Standard Bank, Nedbank and Capitec are all supported), then upload it. OneTouch automatically matches each transaction to your invoices, calculates your match rate, and flags anything unresolved. When you hit a high enough match rate, you can lock the month — creating an immutable audit trail for SARS.
How does the AI bank transaction analysis work? ▼
Export your transactions as CSV or Excel from your bank's transaction history and upload the file. Our AI reads every line — it never guesses. It categorises transactions, calculates VAT on claimable items, and flags anything that could trigger a SARS audit. If it can't read a value clearly, it leaves it blank and tells you to fill it in manually.
Is my financial data safe? ▼
Yes. All data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security — meaning only you can access your data. We are POPIA compliant. We do not sell your data to any third parties, ever.
Can my accountant access my data? ▼
Yes — on the Full plan you can generate a secure, time-limited accountant access link. This gives your accountant read access to your financials without sharing your login. The link expires after 30 days and can be revoked at any time.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes, at any time. If you cancel, your subscription ends at the close of your billing period. All your data is retained — nothing is deleted. You can re-upgrade whenever you're ready.
What tax types does OneTouch support? ▼
OneTouch currently supports VAT (VAT201), PAYE (EMP201), Provisional Tax (IRP6) and Corporate Income Tax preparation (ITR14). Personal income tax support is available via our free Personal Tax app. We are continually adding new features.
Can I manage more than one business on one account? ▼
Each OneTouch account supports one business. If you own multiple businesses, you will need a separate account (and separate subscription) for each one. This keeps your books, invoices and tax records completely separate per entity — which is also what SARS requires. Multi-business support is on our roadmap for a future release.
